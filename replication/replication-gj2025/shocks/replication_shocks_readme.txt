Replication of structural shock identification used in Georgiadis and Jarocinski (2025), Global spillovers from multi-dimensional US monetary policy, Journal of International Economics (forthcoming).

Output of the replication:

- U.csv: point estimates of the shocks (u1: Conventional Monetary Policy, u2: Forward Guidance (Odyssean), u3: LSAP, u4: Information/Delphic Forward Guidance)

- shock_dist.mat: 2000 draws from the posterior distribution of the shocks

To replicate U.csv and shock_dist.mat run Matlab script code/main.m. This script will save U.csv, shock_dist.mat and various plots reporting the simulation, into the folder results/.
